How To Conserve Forest Resources






How to conserve Forest Resources?
1. Maintain a regulated and planned cutting of trees.
2. Have a control over forest fire. According to an estimate, during the period from 1940 to 1950, in the US alone, fires consumed an average of 21.5 million acres of timber yearly.
3. Support Reforestation and Afforestation.
4. Carry out farm rotation methods to retain the fertility of land.
5. Prevent use of chemical fertilizers and pesticides.
6. Take strict actions against overgrazing.
7. As a common man, you may not be able to do any of the above but you can definitely do all of the below:
a. Grow as many plants you can in your house.
b. Grow trees in your society, neighbourhood, backyard.
c. Gift a plant to somebody. Believe us, they will love it!
d. Planting a plant based on your zodiac sign may provide you astrological benefits.
e. There are many NGOs who carry out plantation programs. Take part in those.
