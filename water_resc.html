How To Conserve Water Resources






How to conserve Water Resources?
1. Fill bucket while showering & use it to water plants.
2. Turn off the faucet while brushing your teeth or shaving. Use faucet aerators.
3. Use modern dual-flush systems. Using this can reduce water usage up to 7-8 L per day which can suffice a day’s need for a family in Ghana.
4. Eat less meat! Yes, rearing animals and dairy is a water-intensive occupation. By cutting down your meat & dairy use, you not only save water but also protect climate by preventing global warming.
5. Catch rainwater. By just storing rainwater, you collect in average 300 L in just one hour!
6. Close open taps. There is no good in blaming others for it.
7. Wash cloths only when the washing machine is full.

….We never know the worth of water till the well is dry.
