Role of an Individual to Conserve Natural Resources








Today, we are on the verge of experiencing a universal threat of depletion and contamination of natural resources. So, rather than pointing fingers, it is time to take a stand and come up with innovative, effective and pocket friendly solutions to conserve them. These ideas should be simple which promote people to take steps and make trivial changes in their daily lifestyle which would create a strong impact to protect our ecosystem and biodiversity.

Our Right


Right to Water & Sanitation by UN states that every person has the right to have access for personal and domestic uses between 50-100 L per day which must be safe, acceptable, affordable, physically accessible within 1km of home and collection time of less than 30 minutes and costing of less than 3% of household income.
Right to food is a human right protecting the right for people to feed themselves in dignity, implying that sufficient food is available.
