How To Conserve Energy Resources






How to conserve Energy Resources?
1. Replace all bulbs and with LED light bulbs.
2. Purchase energy efficient appliances.
3. Install solar panels in your house or societies
4. Use renewable sources of energy and support its use.
5. Use electricity only when needed.
6. Unplug electronics when not in use. Many may not know but even in the off or stand-by mode, these appliances suck unintended electric energy and add up to 10% of your annual electric bill.
7. Use washing machines and dishwashers only when full.
8. Conserving energy is usually about cutting down, but sometimes it is also about filling up. It is actually to keep your fridge full as it consumes less energy when it is well stocked.
9. Switching of a computer rather than keeping it in screensaver mode is energy friendly.
10. On the contrary, keep your laptops in sleep or hibernation mode when not in use.
